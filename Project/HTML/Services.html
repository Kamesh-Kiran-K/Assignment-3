Parking

The most common types of long-term parking at airports include terminal parking, economy parking lots, hotel airport parking, and off-site airport parking. These options are suitable for travelers who require extended parking and offer different benefits in terms of cost, proximity to the airport.

Dining Options

We offer free snacks and other food choices, plus drinks, including alcoholic beverages. These food and drink offerings come at no extra cost to you. An airport lounge is a good stop if you're feeling hungry before boarding the plane and don't want to overpay for a meal.

Transportation

Airport transportation services are specialized services designed to transport travelers to and from airports. These services are particularly convenient for passengers who need reliable and hassle-free transportation to or from an airport.

Wi-Fi Availability

The Wi-Fi is available in all terminals of the airport and can be accessed by connecting to the “IGI-Connect” or “GMR Free Wi-Fi” network. However, the speed and quality of the Wi-Fi may vary, and it may not always be reliable.

Shopping
